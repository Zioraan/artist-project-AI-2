About Me

I am a multidisciplinary artist working across acrylic, charcoal, and found materials. My process starts with field sketches and written notes, then evolves into layered compositions that reflect place, light, and personal history.

Based in the UK, I collaborate with local creative communities and mentor emerging painters through seasonal studio workshops.

Career

Over the last decade, my work has been exhibited in independent galleries, residency programs, and regional art fairs.

  • 2025: Featured in the New Voices Contemporary Showcase
  • 2023: Recipient of the Riverlight Studio Residency
  • 2021: Solo exhibition, Tidal Memory, at North Hall Gallery

I continue to develop new collections focused on ecology, landscape, and material experimentation.
